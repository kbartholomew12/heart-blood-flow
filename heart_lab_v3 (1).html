Superior & Inferior Vena Cavae → Right Atrium
Deoxygenated blood from the entire body returns via two great veins. The Superior Vena Cava (SVC) drains the head, neck, and upper extremities. The Inferior Vena Cava (IVC) drains the abdomen, pelvis, and legs into the low-pressure Right Atrium.
